WRITTEN BY DAVID MA

Rupa, the voice behind "Aaj Shanibar," speaks out after forty years

Not until about three minutes in do we hear Rupa's voice wash through the music. The song "Aaj Shanibar" is a showpiece off Disco Jazz, an Indian dance record with an array of influences woven throughout. Not quite disco, but it's become a staple on many upbeat playlists. Nor is it jazz, but peppered

throughout are sparkling solos and complex, shifting arrangements. Disco Jazz is also a capsule of yesteryear and daydreams, shelved in a dusty attic for almost four decades before a boy named Dedayan uncovered it while doing chores.
